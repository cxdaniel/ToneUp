## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is ToneUp free?
Yes! ToneUp offers a generous free tier with core learning features. ToneUp Pro is available for users who want advanced features and unlimited access.

### Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Absolutely! Cancel through your App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android) subscriptions at any time. No questions asked.

### Do I need an internet connection?
Some features work offline, but an internet connection is recommended for the best experience and to sync your progress.

### What Chinese dialect does ToneUp teach?
ToneUp focuses on Standard Mandarin Chinese (Putonghua), which is the official language of China and most widely spoken Chinese dialect.

### Is ToneUp suitable for beginners?
Yes! ToneUp is designed for learners of all levels, from complete beginners to advanced students.

### How long does it take to learn Chinese with ToneUp?
Learning speed varies by individual. With consistent daily practice (15-30 minutes), most users see significant progress within 3-6 months.
